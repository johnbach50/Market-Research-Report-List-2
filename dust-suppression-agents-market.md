Wet dust suppression agents are chemical solutions that are sprayed onto dust particles to make them heavier, causing them to settle down. Dry dust suppression agents, on the other hand, are applied in powder form to absorb moisture from the air and bind with dust particles to prevent them from becoming airborne. Both types of agents help improve air quality and reduce health risks associated with dust exposure.</p></p>
