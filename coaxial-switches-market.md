<p><p>Coaxial switches are categorized based on the number of input/output ports they have. SPnT switches, or Single Pole n Throw switches, have one input port and n output ports. SPDT switches, or Single Pole Double Throw switches, have one input port and two output ports. DPDT switches, or Double Pole Double Throw switches, have two input ports and two output ports. Other types of coaxial switches may have different configurations and numbers of input/output ports to suit specific applications in the market.</p></p>
