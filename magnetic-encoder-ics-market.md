<p><p>Magnetic Encoder ICs are divided into two main types: Linear Encoder ICs and Rotary Encoder ICs. Linear Encoder ICs are used to measure linear motion along a single axis, providing precise position feedback for applications such as linear motors and stages. On the other hand, Rotary Encoder ICs measure rotation around a central axis, commonly used in applications like robotics, CNC machines, and industrial control systems. Both types of Magnetic Encoder ICs offer high accuracy and reliability in motion control systems.</p></p>
