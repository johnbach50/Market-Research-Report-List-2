<p><p>Aromatherapy diffusers come in three main types: Ultrasonic, Nebulizer, and Evaporative. Ultrasonic diffusers use water and essential oils to create a fine mist that is released into the air. Nebulizer diffusers use pressurized air to break down essential oils into tiny particles for a more concentrated scent. Evaporative diffusers use a fan to blow air through a pad or filter containing essential oils. Each type of diffuser has its own unique benefits and uses in the aromatherapy market.</p></p>
