<p><strong>The Commercial Beer Dispensers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Draw System</li><li>Air Cooled System</li><li>Glycol Cooled System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial beer dispensers come in three main types: Direct Draw System, Air Cooled System, and Glycol Cooled System. Direct Draw Systems are the most basic and affordable option, using a built-in refrigeration unit to cool the beer. Air Cooled Systems use a fan to circulate cold air around the beer lines, keeping the beer cool. Glycol Cooled Systems are the most advanced, using a separate glycol chiller to cool the beer lines, ensuring consistent temperature control and preventing foaming. Each system has its own benefits and is suited for different types of establishments.</p></p>
