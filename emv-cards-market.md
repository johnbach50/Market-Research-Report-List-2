<p><strong>The EMV Cards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact-based EMV Cards</li><li>Contactless EMV Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EMV cards are categorized into two main markets: contact-based and contactless. Contact-based EMV cards require physical insertion into a card reader for transactions to occur, providing a higher level of security. On the other hand, contactless EMV cards use near-field communication technology to enable transactions without swiping or inserting the card, making transactions faster and more convenient. Both types of EMV cards offer enhanced security features compared to traditional magnetic stripe cards, reducing the risk of fraud.</p></p>
