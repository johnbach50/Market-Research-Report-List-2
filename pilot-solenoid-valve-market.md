<p><p>Pilot solenoid valves are categorized into three main types: Two-way solenoid valves, Three-way solenoid valves, and Four-way solenoid valves. Two-way solenoid valves control the flow of a fluid in a single direction, while Three-way solenoid valves can handle the flow of fluid in two directions. Four-way solenoid valves are capable of controlling the flow of fluid in four different directions. Each type of solenoid valve serves different purposes and is designed to meet specific requirements in various industries.</p></p>
