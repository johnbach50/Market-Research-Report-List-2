<p><p>Hall sensors are devices that detect the presence of a magnetic field and produce an output signal accordingly. The market for Hall sensors can be classified into two main types: analog output Hall sensors and digital output Hall sensors. Analog output Hall sensors provide a continuous output signal that varies in response to changes in the magnetic field, while digital output Hall sensors produce a discrete signal that switches between two states, typically high and low, based on the presence or absence of a magnetic field.</p></p>
