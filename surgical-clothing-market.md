Reusable clothing includes scrubs, lab coats, and surgical drapes that can be washed and sterilized for multiple uses, reducing the environmental impact and cost. Both types of surgical clothing play a crucial role in maintaining hygiene and preventing the spread of infections in healthcare settings.</p></p>
