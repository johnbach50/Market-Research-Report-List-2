Rotary blade drones, also known as quadcopters or multirotors, have multiple blades that provide vertical takeoff and landing capabilities, as well as superior maneuverability and stability. They are commonly used for tasks requiring close-range operations such as aerial photography, inspections, and search and rescue missions.</p></p>
