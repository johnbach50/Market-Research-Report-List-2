<p><p>Industrial valves market consists of various types such as ball valves, globe valves, gate valves, butterfly valves, check valves, and others. Ball valves offer quick shut-off capabilities, while globe valves are suitable for regulating flow. Gate valves are used in fully open or closed positions, and butterfly valves control flow using a circular disc. Check valves prevent backflow of fluids. The "other" category includes specialty valves like pressure relief valves and diaphragm valves, catering to specific industrial requirements.</p></p>
