<p><p>The Backlight Module Optical Film market includes different types of optical films such as Reflective Film, Diffusion of Membrane, and Lighten the Membrane. Reflective Film is used to reflect light, improving the brightness and efficiency of the backlight module. Diffusion of Membrane helps to scatter light evenly across the screen, reducing glare and improving viewing angles. Lighten the Membrane is designed to increase the brightness and enhance the color saturation of the display. Each type serves a specific purpose in enhancing the performance of backlight modules.</p></p>
