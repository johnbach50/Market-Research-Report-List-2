<p><p>Vehicles DPF Retrofit market includes two main types of diesel particulate filters: Cordierite DPF and Silicon Carbide (SiC) DPF. Cordierite DPFs are more commonly used due to their lower cost and good filtration efficiency. On the other hand, SiC DPFs are known for their higher thermal stability and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty vehicles. Both types of DPFs play a crucial role in reducing harmful emissions from diesel vehicles and are widely adopted in the aftermarket for retrofitting older vehicles to meet stricter environmental regulations.</p></p>
