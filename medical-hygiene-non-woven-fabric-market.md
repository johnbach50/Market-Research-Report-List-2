<p><strong>The Medical Hygiene Non Woven Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drylaid</li><li>Airlaid</li><li>Wetlaid</li><li>Spunlaid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical hygiene non-woven fabric market consists of various types including drylaid, airlaid, wetlaid, and spunlaid. Drylaid fabric is produced by mechanically entangling fibers without the use of liquid, while airlaid fabric involves the use of air to form a web. Wetlaid fabric is created by blending fibers in a water-based solution, and spunlaid fabric is made through a process of extruding fibers onto a moving belt. Each type of fabric offers unique properties and is used in different applications within the medical hygiene industry.</p></p>
