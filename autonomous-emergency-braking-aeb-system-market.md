<p><p>Autonomous Emergency Braking (AEB) Systems utilize various technologies to detect potential collisions and automatically apply the brakes to prevent or reduce the severity of accidents. The four main types of AEB systems are Camera-based, Fusion (combining multiple sensors), LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging), and Radar-based systems. Camera-based systems use visual recognition to detect obstacles, Fusion systems combine camera, radar, and LiDAR technologies for more accurate detection, LiDAR systems use laser light to create 3D maps of the surroundings, while Radar systems use radio waves to detect objects.</p></p>
