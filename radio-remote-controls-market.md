<p><p>Radio remote controls come in two main types: pushbutton and joystick. Pushbutton controls are simple and easy to use, with each button corresponding to a specific function or action. They are commonly used in industrial applications where precise control and quick response are necessary. Joystick controls, on the other hand, offer more flexibility and allow for smooth and continuous movement in multiple directions. They are often used in applications where fluid and precise control over machinery or equipment is required.</p></p>
