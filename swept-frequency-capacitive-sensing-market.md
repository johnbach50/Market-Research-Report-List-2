<p><strong>The Swept Frequency Capacitive Sensing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Capacitance</li><li>Projected Capacitance</li><li>Self-Capacitance</li><li>Mutual Capacitance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Swept Frequency Capacitive Sensing technology is used in various market types including surface capacitance, projected capacitance, self-capacitance, and mutual capacitance. </p><p>Surface capacitance involves measuring changes in capacitance on a surface to detect touch. Projected capacitance uses electrodes to create a grid of capacitors for precise touch sensing. Self-capacitance measures changes in capacitance of a single electrode to detect proximity or touch. Mutual capacitance uses multiple electrodes to detect touch by measuring changes in capacitance between them. Each market type has unique applications and advantages in touch sensing technologies.</p></p>
