<p><strong>The Mica Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Ground</li><li>Dry Ground</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mica powder is available in two main types in the market - wet ground and dry ground. Wet ground mica powder is produced by grinding mica flakes in water to maintain its natural purity and brightness. On the other hand, dry ground mica powder is produced by grinding mica flakes in a specially designed mill without any water content, resulting in a more shimmering and lustrous appearance. Both types are widely used in various industries such as cosmetics, paints, plastics, and construction.</p></p>
