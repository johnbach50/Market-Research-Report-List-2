<p><p>Scalable Data Center Wired Switch market includes two main types: Modular Ethernet switches and Fixed Configuration Ethernet switches. Modular switches are designed with separate components that can be customized and upgraded based on specific needs, offering flexibility and scalability for ever-changing data center requirements. Fixed configuration switches come pre-built with a fixed number of ports and configurations, providing cost-effective solutions for smaller-scale data centers with less complex networking needs. Both types cater to different scales and requirements within the data center ecosystem.</p></p>
