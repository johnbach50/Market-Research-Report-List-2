<p><strong>The Photoresist (PR) Strippers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist (PR) Strippers</li><li>Negative Photoresist (PR) Strippers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photoresist (PR) strippers are used in the semiconductor industry to remove photoresist material after photolithography processes. Positive PR strippers are designed to remove positive photoresist material, which becomes soluble when exposed to light. Negative PR strippers, on the other hand, are formulated to remove negative photoresist material, which becomes insoluble when exposed to light. Both types of PR strippers play a crucial role in the fabrication of integrated circuits and other electronic components by allowing for precise patterning and etching of materials.</p></p>
