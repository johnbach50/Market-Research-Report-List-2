<p><strong>The Rice Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extraction</li><li>Squeezing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rice oil market can be classified into two main types: extraction and squeezing. Extraction involves using chemical solvents to extract oil from rice bran, resulting in higher yields but potentially lower quality. Squeezing, on the other hand, is a more traditional method where oil is pressed out of rice bran mechanically, resulting in a purer and more natural product. Both markets cater to different consumer preferences, with extraction being more common in larger scale commercial productions and squeezing being favored by those seeking a more natural option.</p></p>
