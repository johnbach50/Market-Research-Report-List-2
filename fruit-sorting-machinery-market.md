<p><p>Fruit sorting machinery market types include optical sorting machines, gravity/weight sorting machines, and others. Optical sorting machines use imaging technology to detect defects and sort fruits based on color, size, and shape. Gravity/weight sorting machines use the principle of weight to separate fruits based on their mass. Other types of fruit sorting machinery include automated robotic systems and sensor-based sorting machines. Each type of machinery offers unique advantages in terms of accuracy, efficiency, and speed in the fruit sorting process.</p></p>
