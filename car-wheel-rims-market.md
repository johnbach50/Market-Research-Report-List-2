<p><p>Car wheel rims are available in two main market types: Tubeless Wheel Rims and Tube Wheel Rims. Tubeless wheel rims have become increasingly popular due to their advanced technology that eliminates the need for an inner tube, reducing the risk of punctures and providing better performance. On the other hand, tube wheel rims require an inner tube to hold the air pressure, making them more traditional but still widely used in certain vehicle models. Both types cater to different preferences and needs in the automotive industry.</p></p>
