<p><ul><li>Full-Floating Axle Parts</li><li>Semi-Floating Axle Parts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive axle parts market is divided into two main types: Full-Floating Axle Parts and Semi-Floating Axle Parts. Full-Floating Axle Parts are designed to bear the weight of the vehicle, providing better load-carrying capacity and allowing the axle shaft to rotate freely. On the other hand, Semi-Floating Axle Parts support the weight of the vehicle through the axle casing, with the axle shaft partially supporting the weight. Both types cater to different vehicle requirements and have their own advantages based on load capacity and rotational flexibility.</p></p>
