<p><p>Salt Bath Furnaces are used for various industrial heat treatment processes. Electrically Heated Salt-Bath Furnaces use electric heating elements to heat the salt bath, providing precise and uniform temperatures for the workpiece. Gas-Fired Salt-Bath Furnaces, on the other hand, use burners to heat the salt bath, offering a more cost-effective solution for larger scale operations. Both types of furnaces are widely used in industries such as metalworking, aerospace, and automotive for processes like annealing, tempering, and quenching.</p></p>
