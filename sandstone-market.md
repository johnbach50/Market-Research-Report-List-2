<p><p>Sandstone market types include Quartz Sandstone, Quartzite, and Arkose. Quartz Sandstone is a sedimentary rock composed mainly of quartz grains, known for its durability and weather resistance. Quartzite is a metamorphic rock formed from quartz sandstone, characterized by its hardness and unique patterns. Arkose is a sandstone with high feldspar content, giving it a pink or red hue. Each type of sandstone has different qualities and uses in construction, landscaping, and flooring industries.</p></p>
