<p><p>Absolute optical encoders can be classified into two main types: single-turn encoders and multi-turn encoders. Single-turn encoders provide position information within one complete revolution, while multi-turn encoders can track multiple revolutions, offering more precise position tracking over a larger range of motion. Both types are widely used in various industries such as manufacturing, robotics, and aerospace for applications requiring accurate and reliable position feedback. This segmentation allows customers to choose the encoder type that best fits their specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
