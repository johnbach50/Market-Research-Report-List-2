<p><ul><li>Optical Surgical Navigation Systems</li><li>Electromagnetic Surgical Navigation Systems</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Orthopedic surgery navigation systems consist of various types, including optical surgical navigation systems, electromagnetic surgical navigation systems, and other systems. Optical surgical navigation systems use cameras and infrared light to track instruments and guide surgeons during procedures. Electromagnetic surgical navigation systems utilize electromagnetic fields to provide real-time tracking of instruments within the surgical field. Other navigation systems may include hybrid systems combining different technologies for enhanced accuracy and precision during orthopedic surgeries. These systems aim to improve surgical outcomes and enhance patient recovery.</p></p>
