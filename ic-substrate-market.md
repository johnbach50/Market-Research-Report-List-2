<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The IC Substrate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FC-BGA</li><li>FC-CSP</li><li>WB BGA</li><li>WB CSP</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IC substrates come in various types, including FC-BGA, FC-CSP, WB BGA, WB CSP, and Others. FC-BGA (Flip Chip Ball Grid Array) and FC-CSP (Flip Chip Chip Scale Package) are advanced packaging solutions that offer high performance and compact size. WB BGA (Wire Bond Ball Grid Array) and WB CSP (Wire Bond Chip Scale Package) are traditional packaging options with wire bonding technology. The Others category includes alternative substrate designs and materials tailored to specific customer needs. Each type has its own unique features and benefits for different applications.</p></p>
