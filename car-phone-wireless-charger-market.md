<p><ul><li>Magnetic Wireless Charger</li><li>Non-magnetic Wireless Charger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car phone wireless chargers come in two main types: magnetic and non-magnetic. Magnetic wireless chargers use magnets to securely hold the phone in place while charging, providing a more stable and efficient charging experience. Non-magnetic wireless chargers do not use magnets, but instead rely on other methods such as friction or grips to keep the phone in place. Both types offer the convenience of wirelessly charging your phone while driving, but the choice between magnetic and non-magnetic ultimately depends on personal preference and phone compatibility.</p></p>
