<p><p>Dedicated gluten-free beers are brewed without any gluten-containing ingredients and are safe for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivities. These beers are made in facilities that do not process gluten grains to prevent cross-contamination. On the other hand, gluten-reduced beers are made with gluten-containing grains but undergo a process to reduce the gluten content to below the legal limit for labeling as gluten-free. This allows those with mild gluten sensitivities to enjoy beer with lower gluten levels.</p></p>
