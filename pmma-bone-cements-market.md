<p><p>PMMA bone cements are classified into three types based on their viscosity levels: low viscosity cements, medium viscosity cements, and high viscosity cements. Low viscosity cements are used for vertebroplasty procedures as they provide easy injectability and spreadability. Medium viscosity cements are commonly used for hip and knee arthroplasty due to their balance of handling and strength. High viscosity cements are ideal for load-bearing applications, such as in the spine, as they offer maximum strength and durability.</p></p>
