<p><strong>The TMR Current Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Loop TMR Current Sensor</li><li>Open Loop TMR Current Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Closed loop TMR current sensors are designed to provide precise measurements of current with high accuracy and stability. They feature a feedback loop that continuously adjusts the sensor output to match the input current, ensuring reliable performance. On the other hand, open loop TMR current sensors do not have a feedback mechanism, making them simpler and more cost-effective. However, they may be less accurate and may drift over time. Both types of sensors are widely used in various industries for monitoring and controlling electrical currents.</p></p>
