<p><p>Surfactants market types include anionic surfactants, which are negatively charged and commonly used in household cleaners; amphoteric surfactants, which can be both positively and negatively charged and are often found in personal care products; cationic surfactants, which are positively charged and commonly used in fabric softeners; and nonionic surfactants, which have no charge and are known for their gentle cleansing properties. Each type of surfactant offers unique characteristics and advantages for various applications in industries such as cleaning, personal care, and textiles.</p></p>
