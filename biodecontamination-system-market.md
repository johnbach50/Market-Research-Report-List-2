<p><p>Biodecontamination systems are used to eliminate contaminants in various facilities, with two main market types being chamber decontamination and room decontamination. Chamber decontamination systems are designed for smaller, enclosed spaces like isolators, cleanrooms, and biosafety cabinets. Room decontamination systems, on the other hand, are used for larger spaces such as hospital rooms, laboratories, and pharmaceutical manufacturing facilities. Both types of systems utilize technologies like hydrogen peroxide vapor or chlorine dioxide to effectively decontaminate the space.</p></p>
