<p><p>Industrial Computed Tomography (CT) is a non-destructive testing technique used in various industries to analyze the internal structures of objects. There are three main types of Industrial CT markets - High Energy Industrial CT, Low Energy Industrial CT, and Mini-Focus Industrial CT. High Energy CT is used for inspecting dense materials like metal alloys, Low Energy CT is used for lighter materials such as plastics, and Mini-Focus CT is used for high-resolution imaging of small objects. Each type caters to specific industry needs and materials for effective testing and analysis.</p></p>
