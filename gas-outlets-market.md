<p><p>Gas outlets are available in three main types: wall outlets, console outlets, and ceiling outlets. Wall outlets are installed at waist level, making them easily accessible for connecting gas appliances. Console outlets are located at floor level and are commonly used for connecting portable devices. Ceiling outlets are typically found in commercial settings and are used for connecting gas lines to industrial equipment overhead. Each type of outlet serves a specific purpose based on the location and function of the gas appliance being connected.</p></p>
