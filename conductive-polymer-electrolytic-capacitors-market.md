<p><p>Conductive Polymer Electrolytic Capacitors are available in two main types - Single Ended and Surface Mount. Single Ended capacitors have one electrode exposed while the other is sealed, making them suitable for high ripple current applications. Surface Mount capacitors are designed to be mounted directly onto the surface of a circuit board, saving space and making them suitable for compact electronic devices. Both types offer high performance and reliability, making them ideal for a wide range of applications in the electronics industry.</p></p>
