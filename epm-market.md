<p><p>EPM Market Types - Solution Polymerization and Suspension Polymerization are two methods of producing ethylene propylene monomer (EPM) polymers. Solution Polymerization involves dissolving monomers in a solvent to create a homogeneous solution, while Suspension Polymerization involves dispersing monomers in a liquid medium to form droplets that polymerize into solid particles. Both methods have their advantages and disadvantages, leading to different market segments for EPM products based on their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
