<p><p>Automotive Multimedia Audio Systems can be classified into two main types - UVO Audio and Standard Audio. UVO Audio systems offer advanced features such as touch-screen interfaces, voice recognition, smartphone integration, and Bluetooth connectivity, providing a more interactive and user-friendly experience. On the other hand, Standard Audio systems offer basic audio functions like CD players, AM/FM radios, and auxiliary inputs. UVO Audio systems are considered more high-end and are typically found in luxury vehicles, while Standard Audio systems are more common in mainstream vehicles.</p></p>
