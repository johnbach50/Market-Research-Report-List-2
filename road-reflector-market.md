<p><p>Road reflectors are essential safety devices that help improve visibility on roads, especially at night or in adverse weather conditions. There are two main types of road reflectors: red and yellow. Red reflectors are commonly used to mark the edges of roads and indicate areas where vehicles should not cross, such as the sides of highways or medians. Yellow reflectors are used to highlight the centerline or lane markings on roads, helping drivers stay within their lanes and navigate safely. Both red and yellow reflectors play a crucial role in enhancing road safety and reducing accidents.</p></p>
