<p><ul><li>Die Casting</li><li>Permanent Mold Casting</li><li>Sand Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminium castings market consists of three main types: Die Casting, Permanent Mold Casting, and Sand Casting. Die casting involves injecting molten metal into a mold under high pressure, resulting in intricate shapes with high accuracy. Permanent mold casting utilizes reusable metal molds to create complex shapes with increased dimensional accuracy. Sand casting, on the other hand, involves pouring molten metal into a sand mold, making it suitable for large-scale production and cost-effective for intricate designs.</p></p>
