Biometric card reader-writers verify identity through fingerprint or other biometric data. Magnetic-stripe card reader-writers read information stored on a card's magnetic stripe. Each type caters to specific needs and preferences, ranging from security to convenience in different industries like banking, healthcare, and retail.</p></p>
