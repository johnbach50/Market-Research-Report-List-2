<p><p>Distributed solar power generation includes two main market types: rooftops and ground-mounted systems. Rooftop solar installations are mounted on building rooftops, providing on-site energy generation for the building occupants. Ground-mounted systems are installed on the ground, typically in open fields or solar farms, and connect to the grid to supply electricity to the wider community. Both market types contribute to decentralized electricity generation, reducing reliance on centralized power plants and increasing renewable energy adoption.</p></p>
