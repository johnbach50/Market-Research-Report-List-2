Bent Pin Hitch Locks feature a curved pin that secures the hitch in place, while Flush Pin Hitch Locks have a pin that sits flush with the hitch for added security. Threaded Pin Hitch Locks use a threaded pin mechanism to lock the hitch in place. Each type offers different levels of security and ease of use for protecting trailers and other hitched equipment.</p></p>
