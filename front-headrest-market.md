<p><p>The front headrest market consists of two main types: fixed head restraints and adjustable head restraints. Fixed head restraints are stationary and typically cannot be adjusted to accommodate different preferences. On the other hand, adjustable head restraints can be moved up or down to provide a more customized and comfortable position for the user. Both types serve the purpose of providing support and protection for the head and neck while driving, but offer differing levels of adjustability for user comfort.</p></p>
