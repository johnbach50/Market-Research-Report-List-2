<p><p>Automotive Double Wishbone Suspension System market includes two main types: Non-independent Suspension and Independent Suspension. Non-independent Suspension refers to a system where the movement of one wheel is directly affected by the movement of the opposite wheel on the same axle. Independent Suspension, on the other hand, allows each wheel to move independently, providing better handling, stability, and comfort. Both types of suspension systems are used in various vehicles, depending on the specific requirements and preferences of the manufacturer and consumer.</p></p>
