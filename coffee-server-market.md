<p><p>The coffee server market can be divided into two types: portable and desktop. Portable coffee servers are designed to be easily carried around, making them ideal for travel or outdoor use. They are typically smaller in size and have features like insulated walls and spill-proof lids. On the other hand, desktop coffee servers are larger and are meant to be kept on a stable surface like a table or countertop. They often have a higher capacity and may come with features like a built-in heating element or a brew-through lid.</p></p>
