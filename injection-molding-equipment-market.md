<p><ul><li>Vertical Type</li><li>Horizontal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Injection molding equipment market can be divided into two main types: Vertical and Horizontal. Vertical injection molding machines are ideal for insert molding applications where the mold is stationary and the injection unit moves vertically. They are more space-efficient and can be used for producing smaller parts. On the other hand, horizontal injection molding machines are more versatile and widely used for a variety of applications. They are more suited for producing larger parts and multiple cavity molds. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the specific needs of the manufacturing process.</p></p>
