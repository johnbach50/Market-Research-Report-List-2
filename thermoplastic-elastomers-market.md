<p><p>Thermoplastic elastomers (TPEs) can be categorized into several types based on their chemical composition and properties. Styrene-Based TPEs (SBCs) offer good flexibility and impact resistance. Thermoplastic Polyolefins are known for their durability and weather resistance. Thermoplastic Polyurethanes provide excellent abrasion resistance and are highly versatile. Polyether Ester TPE (TPEE) combines the properties of polyesters and polyethers, offering enhanced flexibility and chemical resistance. Other types of TPEs include copolyesters, polyamides, and blends with other polymers.</p></p>
