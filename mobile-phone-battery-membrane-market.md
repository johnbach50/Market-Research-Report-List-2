<p><p>The mobile phone battery membrane market can be divided into two main types: porous battery membrane and nonporous battery membrane. Porous battery membranes allow for the movement of ions and electrons, enhancing the efficiency and performance of the battery. On the other hand, nonporous battery membranes prevent the leakage of electrolytes and ensure the stability of the battery. Both types play a crucial role in the functionality and longevity of mobile phone batteries.</p></p>
