<p><p>Aerobic septic systems are wastewater treatment systems that rely on oxygen to break down organic matter. The market for these systems includes conventional activated sludge systems, which use microorganisms to treat the wastewater, Moving Bed Biofilm Reactor (MBBR) systems, which use specialized biofilm carriers for microbial growth, and Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) systems, which combine biological treatment with membrane filtration. Each type of system offers unique advantages in terms of treatment efficiency, space requirements, and maintenance.</p></p>
