<p><ul><li>Activated carbon filters</li><li>UV technology</li><li>Reverse Osmosis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water purifier systems come in different types including activated carbon filters, UV technology, and reverse osmosis. Activated carbon filters remove contaminants by trapping them in the porous surface of the carbon. UV technology uses ultraviolet light to kill bacteria and viruses in the water. Reverse osmosis systems force water through a semipermeable membrane to remove impurities. Each type of water purifier system has its own unique benefits and is suitable for different levels of water contamination.</p></p>
