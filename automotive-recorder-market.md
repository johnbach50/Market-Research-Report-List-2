<p><strong>The Automotive Recorder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multi-Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive recorders come in two main types: Single Channel and Multi-Channel. Single Channel recorders capture video and audio from one perspective, typically the front of the vehicle. These are ideal for individual use or for small vehicles. Multi-Channel recorders, on the other hand, capture video and audio from multiple angles simultaneously, providing a more comprehensive view of the surroundings. These are commonly used in commercial vehicles or for fleet management purposes where a complete picture of the vehicle's surroundings is necessary.</p></p>
