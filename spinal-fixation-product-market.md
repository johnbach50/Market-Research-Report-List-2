<p><p>Spinal Fixation Products Market includes two main types: Spinal Fusion and Non-fusion Products. Spinal Fusion involves permanently joining two or more vertebrae, commonly used to treat conditions like spinal stenosis or scoliosis. Non-fusion products provide support to the spine without fusing the vertebrae together, allowing for more flexibility. Each type of product caters to different patient needs and preferences, offering various options for spinal stabilization and alignment. Both types play a crucial role in the treatment of spinal disorders and injuries.</p></p>
