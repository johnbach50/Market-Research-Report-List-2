<p><strong>The SDS Adapter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chuck Adapter</li><li>Flexi-Click Adapter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SDS adapters are accessories used with power tools to allow for compatibility with different types of bits and attachments. Chuck adapters are used to convert SDS drills to standard chuck drills, enabling users to use a wider range of drill bits. Flexi-Click adapters provide versatility by allowing users to quickly change between different types of attachments, such as drill chucks, bit holders, and angle adapters. Both chuck and Flexi-Click adapters enhance the functionality and usability of power tools in various applications.</p></p>
