<p><p>Politetrefluoroethylene (PTFE) is available in different market types such as granular PTFE, fine PTFE, aqueous dispersion PTFE, and micronized PTFE. Granular PTFE is used for compression molding applications, while fine PTFE is suitable for lubrication and coating purposes. Aqueous dispersion PTFE is used in non-stick coatings, and micronized PTFE is ideal for high-performance lubricants and plastics. Each type of PTFE offers specific properties and is used in various industries based on its characteristics.</p></p>
