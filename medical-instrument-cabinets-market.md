<p><p>Medical instrument cabinets come in two main styles: wall-mounted and floor-mounted. Wall-mounted cabinets are attached to the wall and are ideal for saving space in a medical facility. They are also easily accessible for healthcare professionals. Floor-mounted cabinets, on the other hand, stand freely on the floor and provide more storage capacity for medical instruments. They are often used in larger healthcare facilities where space is not limited. Both styles of cabinets are essential for organizing and storing medical instruments effectively.</p></p>
