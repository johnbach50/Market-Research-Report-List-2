<p><p>Automotive composite leaf springs are a type of suspension component used in vehicles to support the weight of the vehicle and provide a smoother ride. There are two main types of markets for automotive composite leaf springs: transversal and longitudinal. Transversal leaf springs are positioned across the width of the vehicle, providing support in a lateral direction. Longitudinal leaf springs, on the other hand, run along the length of the vehicle, offering support in a longitudinal direction. Both types of leaf springs play a crucial role in ensuring the stability and performance of a vehicle's suspension system.</p></p>
