<p><p>Platinum resistive temperature sensors are essential components in various industries for precise temperature measurement. There are two main types of platinum resistive temperature sensors: wire-wound type and thin film type. </p><p>Wire-wound type sensors are made by winding a platinum wire around a ceramic or glass core, providing accurate and stable temperature measurements. On the other hand, thin film type sensors consist of a thin layer of platinum on a substrate, offering fast response times and high sensitivity to temperature changes. Both types play a crucial role in temperature monitoring applications.</p></p>
