<p><strong>The Automotive Occupant Sensing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Mats</li><li>Strain Gauges</li><li>Cameras</li><li>Ultrasonic Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Occupant Sensing System (OSS) consists of different types such as pressure mats, strain gauges, cameras, and ultrasonic sensors. Pressure mats use weight sensors to detect the presence of occupants, while strain gauges measure the deformation caused by an occupant's weight. Cameras capture images to analyze the presence and position of occupants. Ultrasonic sensors emit sound waves to detect occupants based on their location in the vehicle. These different types of OSS help improve safety by ensuring proper deployment of airbags based on the occupants' characteristics.</p></p>
