<p><p>Veterinary treatment ozone therapy units are used for various applications including hyperbaric major autohemotherapy, normobaric minor autohemotherapy, injection and syringe therapy, insufflation, water ozonisation, and others. Hyperbaric major autohemotherapy involves exposing blood to ozone in a pressurized chamber, while normobaric minor autohemotherapy involves direct injections. Injection and syringe therapy involves injecting ozone directly into specific areas. Insufflation involves introducing ozone into the body through the rectum, vagina, or ears. Water ozonisation is the process of sterilizing water using ozone.</p></p>
