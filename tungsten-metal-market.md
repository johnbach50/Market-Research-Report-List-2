<p><p>Tungsten metal market is primarily classified into three types based on the ores used for extraction: Wolframite, Scheelite, and Ferberite markets. Wolframite market involves the extraction of tungsten from the mineral wolframite, which is a solid solution series between the minerals hubnerite and ferberite. Scheelite market focuses on the extraction of tungsten from the mineral scheelite, which is a calcium tungstate mineral. Ferberite market involves the extraction of tungsten from the mineral ferberite, which is an iron-black mineral.</p></p>
