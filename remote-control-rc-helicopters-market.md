Three Channel RC Helicopters are basic models suitable for beginners, with three controls for up, down, left and right movements. Six Channel RC Helicopters are advanced models with added features like hover, side-to-side movement, and complex aerial maneuvers, making them suitable for experienced users.</p></p>
