<p><p>The Aircraft Air Conditioning Market includes two main types - Mobile Type and Floor-Mounted Type. Floor-Mounted Type air conditioning units are fixed to the aircraft's floor and are typically larger in size. They provide more uniform cooling throughout the aircraft cabin and are often used in larger aircraft with more passengers.</p></p>
