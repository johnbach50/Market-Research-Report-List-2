<p><p>Wave filters are essential components in electronic circuits that help to remove unwanted frequencies and noise from signals. Active filters utilize active components like transistors and op-amps to amplify and process signals, providing higher accuracy and efficiency. Passive filters, on the other hand, rely on passive components like resistors, capacitors, and inductors to filter out frequencies, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Both types have their own advantages and applications in various electronic systems, catering to different needs and requirements in the market.</p></p>
