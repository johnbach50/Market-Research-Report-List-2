<p><ul><li>Wet Spinning Acrylic Fibers</li><li>Dry Spinning Acrylic Fibers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acrylic fibers are commonly produced through two main methods: wet spinning and dry spinning. Wet spinning involves dissolving the acrylic polymer in a solvent before extruding it through a spinneret into a coagulating bath to form fibers. Dry spinning, on the other hand, involves dissolving the polymer in a solvent and spinning it directly into a heated chamber to evaporate the solvent and solidify the fibers. Both methods have their own advantages and are used in different applications within the acrylic fibers market.</p></p>
