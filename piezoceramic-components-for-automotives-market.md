<p><p>Piezoceramic components for automotives come in two main types: single plate and multilayer piezo ceramics. Single plate piezo ceramics are simpler in structure and offer high sensitivity and reliability for applications such as automotive sensors and actuators. Multilayer piezo ceramics consist of multiple layers stacked together, providing higher voltage output and improved performance for demanding automotive applications like fuel injection systems and automotive engine controls. Both types of piezo ceramics play a crucial role in enhancing automotive technology and performance.</p></p>
