<p><p>The paper tubes market can be categorized into two main types: spiral paper tubes and seamless paper tubes. Spiral paper tubes are made by winding multiple layers of paper spirally and are commonly used for packaging and mailing purposes. On the other hand, seamless paper tubes are made from a single piece of paper, providing a smooth and uniform surface. Seamless paper tubes are typically used for applications that require a higher level of precision and strength.</p></p>
