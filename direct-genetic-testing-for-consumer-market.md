<p><p>Direct genetic testing for consumers includes various market types such as diagnostic screening, prenatal testing, newborn screening, and pre-implantation diagnosis. Diagnostic screening involves testing for genetic disorders in adults. Prenatal testing is performed during pregnancy to screen for genetic abnormalities in the fetus. Newborn screening detects genetic disorders in newborns. Pre-implantation diagnosis is used during IVF to select embryos without genetic conditions. Relationship testing is also available to determine biological relationships between individuals.</p></p>
