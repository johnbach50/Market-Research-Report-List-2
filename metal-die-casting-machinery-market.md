<p><p>Metal die casting machinery market consists of two main types: hot chamber die casting machines and cold chamber die casting machines. Hot chamber machines are used for low melting point metals like zinc and are efficient for high volume production. Cold chamber machines are used for high melting point metals like aluminum and are suitable for producing complex parts. Both types of machines play a crucial role in the metal die casting industry, catering to different material requirements and production volumes.</p></p>
