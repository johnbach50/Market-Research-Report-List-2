<p><p>The GDI system market includes different components such as fuel injectors, engine control equipment, sensors, fuel rails, and fuel pumps. Fuel injectors deliver gasoline directly into the combustion chamber for improved fuel efficiency and power output. Engine control equipment regulates the injection process for optimal performance. Sensors monitor various parameters for efficient operation. Fuel rails distribute fuel to each injector, while fuel pumps supply fuel from the tank to the system. All these components work together to enhance the performance of the GDI system in vehicles.</p></p>
