<p><p>A concrete form release agent is a substance applied to molds and forms before pouring concrete to prevent the concrete from sticking to the forms. <p><p>Concrete Form Release Agent Market Types include Internal Mold Releases and External Mold Releases. Internal Mold Releases are used to prevent concrete from sticking to the molds and are applied directly to the mold surface. External Mold Releases are applied to the surface of the concrete before molds are placed to facilitate easy removal of the molds after the concrete has cured. Both types of form release agents help in achieving smooth and clean concrete finishes.</p></p>
