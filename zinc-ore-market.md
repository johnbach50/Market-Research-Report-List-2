<p><p>The Zinc Ore market can be divided into four main types: Sphalerite, Siliceous Ore, Manganese-zinc Ore, and Other ores. Sphalerite is the most common zinc ore and is typically found in association with galena and pyrite. Siliceous Ores contain high levels of silica and are often harder to process. Manganese-zinc Ores contain significant levels of manganese in addition to zinc. Other types of zinc ores include smithsonite, hemimorphite, and willemite, each with their own unique characteristics and processing challenges.</p></p>
