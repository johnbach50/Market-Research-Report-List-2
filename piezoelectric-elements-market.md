<p><strong>The Piezoelectric Elements Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PZT-based</li><li>PMN-based</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Piezoelectric elements market can be categorized into three main types: PZT-based, PMN-based, and Others. PZT-based elements utilize lead zirconate titanate materials and are commonly used in various applications including sensors and actuators. PMN-based elements are made from lead magnesium niobate materials and are known for their high performance in sensing and actuation applications. The third category, Others, includes elements made from alternative materials that offer unique properties and capabilities. Each type of piezoelectric element caters to different industry needs and applications.</p></p>
