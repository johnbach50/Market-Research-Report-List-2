<p><p>Electrical Variable Optical Attenuators (EVOA) are commonly available in the market in two types: Single Channel and 4 Channel. Single channel EVOAs are designed to control the attenuation of light in a single optical channel, while 4 channel EVOAs offer the ability to simultaneously control the attenuation in four different optical channels. Both types of EVOAs provide precision control over the amount of light passing through, making them essential components in telecommunications, data centers, and fiber optic networks.</p></p>
