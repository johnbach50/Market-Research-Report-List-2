<p><p>The edge emitting diode lasers market consists of various types of lasers including Distributed Feedback Laser, Distributed Bragg Reflector Laser, Fabry-Perot Laser, and Broad Area Laser Diodes. Distributed Feedback Lasers offer single-mode operation, low threshold current, and narrow linewidth. Distributed Bragg Reflector Lasers provide high output power and spectral purity. Fabry-Perot Lasers have a simple structure and are cost-effective. Broad Area Laser Diodes offer high power output but with lower beam quality. These different types cater to specific applications and requirements within the edge emitting diode lasers market.</p></p>
