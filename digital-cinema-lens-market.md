<p><p>There are two main types of digital cinema lenses - Prime Lens and Zoom Lens. Prime lenses have a fixed focal length, offering superior image quality and wider aperture, making them ideal for capturing crisp and detailed shots in low light conditions. Zoom lenses, on the other hand, have a variable focal length, allowing the user to zoom in and out without changing the lens, making them versatile for different shooting scenarios. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of filmmakers in the digital cinema industry.</p></p>
