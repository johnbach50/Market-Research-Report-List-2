<p><ul><li>Non-Screen Type Films</li><li>Screen Type Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial X-ray flaw detection films are classified into two main types: non-screen type films and screen type films. </p><p>Non-screen type films are used for direct exposure to X-ray radiation without the need for an intensifying screen. They are cost-effective and provide high contrast images but require longer exposure times. </p><p>Screen type films include an intensifying screen that enhances image quality and reduces exposure time. They are more sensitive to X-ray radiation but can be more costly. Both types of films play a crucial role in detecting flaws in industrial materials through X-ray imaging.</p></p>
