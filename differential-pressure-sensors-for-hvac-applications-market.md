Digital differential pressure sensors offer precise and accurate readings in digital format, making them easy to interface with other digital systems. Analog differential pressure sensors provide real-time output in the form of voltage or current signals, allowing for continuous monitoring and control of HVAC systems. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in maintaining optimal pressure levels in HVAC installations to ensure efficient and effective operation.</p></p>
