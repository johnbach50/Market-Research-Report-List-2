<p><p>EAA Adhesive market is divided into three main types: Film Grade, Extrusion Coating Grade, and Dispersible Grade. Film Grade EAA adhesives are used in packaging and labeling applications due to their excellent adhesion properties. Extrusion Coating Grade EAA adhesives are commonly used in food packaging and lamination applications. Dispersible Grade EAA adhesives are designed to disperse easily in water, making them suitable for industries such as construction and textiles. Each type caters to specific needs in various industries, providing strong bonding capabilities.</p></p>
