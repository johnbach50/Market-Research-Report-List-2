<p><p>The frac plugs market consists of three main types: Composite Plugs, Dissolvable Plugs, and Cast Iron Plugs. Composite plugs are made from a combination of materials such as ceramics and metals, providing high strength and durability. Dissolvable plugs are designed to dissolve over time, reducing the need for intervention post-frac. Cast Iron Plugs are traditional plugs made from cast iron, known for their strength and reliability. Each type of plug offers different advantages, catering to various well completion requirements in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
