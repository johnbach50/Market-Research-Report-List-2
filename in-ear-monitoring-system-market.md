<p><p>In-ear monitoring systems are used by musicians and performers to hear themselves clearly during live performances. There are two main types of in-ear monitoring systems: wired and wireless. Wired systems are connected directly to the audio source, providing a reliable connection but limiting mobility. Wireless systems use radio frequency or Bluetooth technology to transmit audio signals without the need for cables, allowing for greater freedom of movement on stage. Both types of systems offer advantages and are used in different scenarios based on the performers' needs.</p></p>
