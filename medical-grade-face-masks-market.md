<p><p>Medical grade face masks market can be classified into two main types - surgical masks and procedure masks. Surgical masks are loose-fitting masks that cover the nose and mouth and are designed to protect against large droplets, splashes, or sprays. Procedure masks, on the other hand, are smaller, more tightly fitting masks that are usually used during medical procedures to protect both the wearer and the patient. Both types of masks are vital in preventing the spread of infections in healthcare settings.</p></p>
