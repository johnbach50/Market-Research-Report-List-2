<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Array junction boxes are categorized into Type I and Type II markets based on their application and features. Type I junction boxes are designed for residential and small commercial solar panel systems, offering basic protection and monitoring capabilities. On the other hand, Type II junction boxes are more advanced and are suitable for larger commercial or utility-scale installations, providing enhanced features like remote monitoring, string-level control, and higher voltage handling capacity. Both types cater to different market segments with varying requirements and complexities.</p></p>
