<p><p>The Intelligent Electronic Devices market consists of various types of products such as Digital Relays, PLC (Programmable Logic Controllers), Load Tap Controllers, Reclosers, Smart Meters, and others. Digital Relays are protective devices used in power systems to detect abnormal conditions and trigger circuit breakers. PLCs are used for automation and control of industrial processes. Load Tap Controllers adjust voltage levels in power transformers. Reclosers help in automatically restoring power after a temporary fault. Smart Meters enable two-way communication between utilities and consumers for efficient energy management.</p></p>
