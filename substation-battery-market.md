<p><p>Substation battery market types include nickel-cadmium batteries, lead-acid batteries, sealed lead-acid batteries, and ventilated lead-acid batteries. Nickel-cadmium batteries are known for their long lifespan and high tolerance for extreme temperatures. Lead-acid batteries are the most commonly used type due to their cost-effectiveness and reliability. Sealed lead-acid batteries are maintenance-free and suitable for indoor use. Ventilated lead-acid batteries require regular maintenance but offer higher energy capacity. Other types of batteries in the market may include lithium-ion batteries or flow batteries, which provide different advantages such as higher energy density or longer cycle life.</p></p>
