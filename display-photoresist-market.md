<p><p>Display photoresist market types include Color Resists (RGB) and Black Matrix (BM). Color resists are used to create vibrant and accurate colors in displays by layering red, green, and blue photoresists to form pixels. Black Matrix photoresists, on the other hand, are used to create the black grid lines around pixels to enhance contrast and improve image clarity. Both types play a crucial role in the manufacturing of high-quality displays for various electronic devices.</p></p>
