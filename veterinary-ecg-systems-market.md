<p><p>Veterinary ECG systems come in various types, including resting ECG, which is used for routine cardiac evaluations in calm animals. Holter ECG systems allow for continuous monitoring of heart activity over extended periods, providing valuable data on irregularities during the animal's daily activities. Other types of ECG systems in the market may include stress ECG or exercise ECG, which measure heart activity during physical exertion. Each type of ECG system serves a specific purpose in monitoring and diagnosing cardiac conditions in animals.</p></p>
