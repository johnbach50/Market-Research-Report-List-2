<p><p>Calibration equipment market is categorized into four main types: Mechanical Calibration Equipment, Electrical Calibration Equipment, Physical/Dimensional Calibration Equipment, and Thermodynamic Calibration Equipment. Mechanical calibration equipment is used for measuring pressure, force, torque, and vibration. Electrical calibration equipment calibrates electronic instruments like oscilloscopes and multimeters. Physical/dimensional calibration equipment is used for length, width, and height measurements. Thermodynamic calibration equipment is used for temperature and heat flow measurements. Each type of equipment plays a crucial role in ensuring accurate and precise measurements in various industries.</p></p>
