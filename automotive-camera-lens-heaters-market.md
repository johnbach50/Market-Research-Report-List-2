Silicone heaters provide uniform heating over large surfaces and are highly durable. Polyimide heaters are thin, lightweight, and flexible, making them ideal for small and compact camera applications. Other types of heaters used in the automotive camera lens market include flexible film heaters and thick film heaters. These heaters are designed to prevent condensation and frost formation on camera lenses, ensuring clear visibility while driving.</p></p>
