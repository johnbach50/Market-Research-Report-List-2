<p><p>The cosmetic grade pigments market is categorized into two main types: water-soluble pigments and oil-soluble pigments. Water-soluble pigments are colorants that easily dissolve in water-based formulations, such as lotions and gels, providing vibrant and long-lasting color. On the other hand, oil-soluble pigments are designed to be mixed with oil-based products like lipsticks and eyeliners, offering stable and intense colors. Both types of pigments are widely used in the cosmetics industry to enhance the visual appeal of various products.</p></p>
