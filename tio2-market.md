<p><p>TiO2 market is categorized into two main types: Sulfate Process and Chloride Process. The Sulfate Process involves the use of sulfuric acid to produce TiO2, while the Chloride Process utilizes chlorine gas. The Sulfate Process is more common and cost-effective, but the Chloride Process offers higher quality and purity of TiO2. However, the Chloride Process is more expensive and complex. Both processes cater to different market demands and applications, with the Sulfate Process being more widely used in various industries.</p></p>
