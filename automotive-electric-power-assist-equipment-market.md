<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Power Assist Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Column EPS</li><li>Pinion EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Electric Power Assist Equipment (EPAS) typically comes in two main types: Column EPS and Pinion EPS. Column EPS refers to a system where the assist motor is located directly on the steering column, providing assistance to the driver in turning the wheel. On the other hand, Pinion EPS involves the assist motor being mounted on the steering rack itself, aiding in steering by applying torque to the steering gear. Both systems offer improved handling and control for drivers, enhancing the overall driving experience.</p></p>
