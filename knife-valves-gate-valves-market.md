<p><p>Knife valves and gate valves are two types of valves commonly used in industrial applications. Knife valves have a sharp-edge gate that slides across the flow stream to control the flow of liquid or gas. They are ideal for applications requiring precise flow control. Gate valves on the other hand have a flat or wedge-shaped gate that either opens or closes the flow stream. They are commonly used in applications where a tight seal is required. Both knife valves and gate valves are essential in regulating flow in various industries.</p></p>
