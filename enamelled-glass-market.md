<p><p>Enamelled glass is available in three main types: transparent, translucent, and opaque. Transparent enamelled glass allows light to pass through clearly, making it ideal for applications where natural light is desired. Translucent glass diffuses light, providing privacy while still allowing some light to pass through. Opaque enamelled glass blocks all light transmission and offers complete privacy. Each type of enamelled glass has its own unique characteristics and applications, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
