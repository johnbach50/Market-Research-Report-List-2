<p><strong>The Veterinary RFID Readers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed RFID Readers</li><li>Handheld RFID Readers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fixed RFID Readers are stationary devices that are installed in specific locations within veterinary clinics or hospitals to read RFID tags on animals passing by. They are typically used for tracking and managing animal records, as well as for security purposes. On the other hand, Handheld RFID Readers are portable devices that can be carried around by veterinarians or other staff members for on-the-go scanning of animal tags. This allows for real-time tracking and monitoring of animals in different locations within the facility.</p></p>
