ANC earbuds use advanced technology to actively block out external noise, providing a more immersive listening experience. On the other hand, Passive Noise Canceling earbuds rely on physical barriers to reduce noise, such as snug-fitting ear tips. Both types aim to improve sound quality by minimizing background noise.
