<p><p>LED lighting, or light-emitting diode lighting, is a type of lighting technology that is much more energy-efficient and long-lasting than traditional incandescent or fluorescent lighting. They also generate less heat, making them safer to use and reducing energy consumption.</p><p>The LED Lighting Market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years due to the rising demand for energy-efficient lighting solutions in residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.
