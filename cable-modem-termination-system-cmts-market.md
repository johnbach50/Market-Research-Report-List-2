<p><p>Cable Modem Termination System (CMTS) market includes two primary types - Integrated CMTS (I-CMTS) and Modular CMTS (M-CMTS). On the other hand, M-CMTS separates the CMTS functions into modular components, allowing for more flexibility and scalability. Both I-CMTS and M-CMTS offer unique features and benefits to cater to the varying needs of cable operators and service providers.</p></p>
