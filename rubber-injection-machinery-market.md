<p><strong>The Rubber Injection Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Type</li><li>Horizontal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rubber injection machinery is classified into two main types: vertical type and horizontal type. Vertical type machinery is designed for applications where the mold needs to be clamped vertically, allowing for easier mold changes and maintenance. On the other hand, horizontal type machinery is ideal for larger molds and applications where the mold needs to be clamped horizontally. Each type offers unique advantages based on the specific requirements of the application, making them suitable for a wide range of industries.</p></p>
