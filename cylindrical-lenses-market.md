<p><p>The cylindrical lenses market consists of various types such as N-BK7, UV fused silica, CaF2, MgF2, BaF2, ZnSe, Si, and others. CaF2 lenses are ideal for infrared applications. MgF2 lenses have low refractive index and dispersion. BaF2 lenses have a wide transmission range. ZnSe lenses are suitable for infrared imaging. Si lenses offer high optical transmission in the infrared spectrum. The market also includes other types like germanium lenses.</p></p>
