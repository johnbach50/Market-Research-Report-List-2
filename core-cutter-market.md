<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Semi-Automatic</li><li>Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Core cutters are available in three main market types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual core cutters require the operator to manually load and cut the cores, making them the most basic and cost-effective option. Semi-automatic core cutters assist the operator in loading the cores but still require manual input for cutting. Automatic core cutters are fully automated, with advanced features such as automatic loading, cutting, and unloading of cores, offering the highest level of efficiency and productivity. Each market type caters to different production requirements and budgets.</p></p>
