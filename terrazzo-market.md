<p><p>Terrazzo is a composite material used for flooring and wall treatments, typically made by mixing chips of marble, quartz, granite, glass, or other materials with a binder. There are two main types of Terrazzo markets: Inorganic Terrazzo, which uses cement as the binder, and Epoxy Terrazzo, which uses epoxy resin. Inorganic Terrazzo is more traditional and has been used for centuries, while Epoxy Terrazzo is a newer, more customizable option that offers greater design flexibility and durability. Both types of Terrazzo are popular choices for commercial and residential spaces.</p></p>
