<p><p>The fire extinguishers market consists of two main types: stored pressure type and cartridge-operated type. Stored pressure extinguishers contain both the extinguishing agent and the propellant within the same container, making them easy to use and maintain. Cartridge-operated extinguishers have a separate cartridge that punctures and mixes with the extinguishing agent when activated. They are typically used for more specialized applications and require more training to use effectively. Both types play a crucial role in fire safety and prevention.</p></p>
