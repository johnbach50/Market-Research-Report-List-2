<p><p>Airport solar power market consists of various types of solar photovoltaic (PV) technologies, including Silicon PV, which is the most commonly used and cost-effective option. Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) PV offers high efficiency but comes at a higher cost. Cadmium Telluride (CdTe) PV is known for its low manufacturing costs. CIS/CIGS PV technology combines copper, indium, gallium, and selenium elements to provide a flexible and lightweight option for airport installations. Each technology caters to different airport requirements based on cost, efficiency, and space constraints.</p></p>
