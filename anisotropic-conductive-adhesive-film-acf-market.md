<p><p>Anisotropic Conductive Adhesive Film (ACF) is used in electronics to make electrical connections between two substrates. Double Layer ACF consists of two layers of conductive adhesive sandwiching a non-conductive layer, allowing for more reliable connections. Three Layers ACF includes an additional insulating layer, further enhancing the electrical conductivity and reliability of connections. Both types of ACF are used in various electronic devices such as LCD screens, flexible circuits, and touch panels, offering improved performance and durability.</p></p>
