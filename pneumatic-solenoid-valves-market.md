<p><p>Pneumatic solenoid valves are classified into various types based on their functional configurations. Two-way SV valves control the flow of air in two directions, while Three-way SV valves manage airflow in three directions. Quad SV valves handle airflow in four directions, offering greater flexibility in pneumatic systems. Other types of solenoid valves include specialty valves designed for specific applications, such as high flow or high pressure situations. Each type of valve plays a unique role in regulating air flow within pneumatic systems.</p></p>
