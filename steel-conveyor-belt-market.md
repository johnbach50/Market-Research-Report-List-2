<p><ul><li>Strand Steel Cord Rubber Conveyor Belts</li><li>Layer Steel Cord Rubber Conveyor Belts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steel conveyor belts are classified into two main types: Strand steel cord rubber conveyor belts and layer steel cord rubber conveyor belts. Strand steel cord belts are made of strands of steel cords embedded in rubber, providing high tensile strength and durability. Layer steel cord belts have multiple layers of steel cords sandwiched between layers of rubber, offering increased flexibility and resistance to impact. Both types are widely used in industries like mining, construction, and food processing for efficient material handling.</p></p>
