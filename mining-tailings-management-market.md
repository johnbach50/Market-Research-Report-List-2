<p><p>Mining tailings management refers to the process of managing and disposing of waste materials generated during mining operations. The market types for tailings management include dry stacking, underground storage, and others. Dry stacking involves storing the tailings in a dry form, reducing the risk of water contamination. Underground storage involves disposing of tailings in underground mines or caverns. Other methods may include the use of tailings ponds or paste backfilling. Each method has its own benefits and considerations for effective tailings management.</p></p>
