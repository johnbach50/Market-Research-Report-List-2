<p><p>HDPE material market includes various types such as Blow Molding, Injection Molding, and Extrusion Molding. Blow molding involves creating hollow products such as bottles by inflating a heated plastic tube inside a mold. Injection molding is a process where molten plastic is injected into a mold to create solid products. Extrusion molding is a continuous process where molten plastic is forced through a die to create long products with a consistent cross-section. Each type caters to different product requirements in the market.</p></p>
