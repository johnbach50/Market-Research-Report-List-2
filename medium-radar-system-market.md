<p><p>CW Radars and Pulsed Radars are two types of Medium Radar Systems used in various industries. CW Radars continuously emit radar signals and are suitable for measuring the velocity of moving objects. Pulsed Radars, on the other hand, emit short pulses of radar signals and are ideal for measuring the distance and direction of objects. Both types have their own advantages and are utilized in applications such as weather monitoring, defense systems, and traffic control.</p></p>
