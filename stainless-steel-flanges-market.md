<p><strong>The Stainless Steel Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Weld Flange</li><li>Blind Flange</li><li>Slip-On Flange</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stainless steel flanges are classified into various types based on their design and usage. Weld flanges are welded to the pipe for a strong connection. Blind flanges are used to seal the end of a pipe or vessel. Slip-on flanges are ideal for low-pressure applications and easy installation. Other types of flanges include socket weld flanges, threaded flanges, and lap joint flanges, each serving a specific purpose in piping systems. These different types cater to various industrial needs and requirements.</p></p>
