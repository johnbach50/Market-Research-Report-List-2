<p><p>Laminated automotive glass is commonly used in four main types of applications in the automotive industry: windscreen, backlite, sidelite, and sunroof. The windscreen is the front glass of the vehicle that protects passengers from debris and provides structural support. The backlite is the rear glass that offers visibility and protection. Sidelites are the side windows that provide ventilation and visibility. Sunroofs are generally located on the roof of the vehicle and can be opened to let in sunlight and fresh air.</p></p>
