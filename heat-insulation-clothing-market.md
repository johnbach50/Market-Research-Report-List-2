<p><p>Heat insulation clothing market includes two main types: split type and siamese type. Split type heat insulation clothing consists of separate top and bottom pieces that can be worn individually or together. Siamese type heat insulation clothing features a one-piece design with the top and bottom connected, providing full body coverage. Split type offers more flexibility and customization, while siamese type provides more overall coverage and protection. Both types cater to different preferences and needs in the heat insulation clothing market.</p></p>
