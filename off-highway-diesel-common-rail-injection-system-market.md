<p><ul><li>Conventional Solenoid Injector</li><li>Piezoelectric Injector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Off-highway Diesel Common Rail Injection System market includes two main types of injectors: Conventional Solenoid Injector and Piezoelectric Injector. Conventional solenoid injectors operate by using an electromagnet to open and close the injector valve, while piezoelectric injectors use a piezoelectric crystal to control fuel delivery. Piezoelectric injectors are known for their precision and ability to deliver multiple injections per combustion cycle, making them more efficient than conventional solenoid injectors in off-highway diesel applications.</p></p>
