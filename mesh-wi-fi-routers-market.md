<p><p>Mesh Wi-Fi Routers come in different market types including Bi-Band, Tri-Band, and Quad-Band. Bi-Band routers operate on two separate frequency bands, typically 2.4GHz and 5GHz, providing basic performance. Tri-Band routers have an additional 5GHz band, offering faster speeds and less congestion. Quad-Band routers have even more frequency bands, providing the fastest speeds and best performance for large homes or offices with many devices. Users can choose the best option based on their specific needs and budget.</p></p>
