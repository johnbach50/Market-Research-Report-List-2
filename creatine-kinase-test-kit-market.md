<p><strong>The Creatine Kinase Test Kit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Enzyme Coupling Method</li><li>Colorimetry Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Creatine Kinase Test Kit Market offers two primary types of testing methods: Enzyme Coupling Method and Colorimetry Method. </p><p>In the Enzyme Coupling Method, the test kit measures creatine kinase levels by detecting the production of a colored product through an enzymatic reaction. This method is commonly used for a quick and accurate measurement of creatine kinase in samples. </p><p>On the other hand, the Colorimetry Method involves measuring the absorbance of a colored product formed during the enzymatic reaction, providing a reliable and cost-effective option for creatine kinase testing.</p></p>
