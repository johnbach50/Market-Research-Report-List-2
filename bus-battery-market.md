<p><strong>The Bus Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LFP</li><li>NMC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LFP (Lithium Iron Phosphate) and NMC (Nickel Manganese Cobalt) are two types of batteries commonly used in the bus battery market. LFP batteries are known for their long lifespan, high thermal stability, and safety, making them ideal for electric buses. On the other hand, NMC batteries offer higher energy density and longer driving range, making them popular in the electric bus market as well. Both types of batteries have their own advantages and are chosen based on specific requirements and priorities of bus manufacturers and operators.</p></p>
