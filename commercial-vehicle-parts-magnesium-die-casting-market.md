<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Parts Magnesium Die Casting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Vacuum Die Casting</li><li>Squeeze Die Casting</li><li>Gravity Die Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure Die Casting, also known as high-pressure die casting, involves injecting molten metal into a steel mold cavity at high pressures. Vacuum Die Casting involves creating a vacuum in the die cavity before the molten metal is injected, ensuring high-quality parts with minimal porosity. Squeeze Die Casting involves applying pressure or force to the molten metal in the die cavity to improve the metal flow and reduce porosity. Gravity Die Casting involves pouring molten metal into a die cavity using the force of gravity.</p></p>
