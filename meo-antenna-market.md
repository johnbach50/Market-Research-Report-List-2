<p><strong>The MEO Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEO (Medium Earth Orbit) antenna market can be classified into two types: Active and Passive. Active MEO antennas require external power sources to amplify signals and enhance performance, making them more suitable for long-distance communication and complex data transmission. On the other hand, Passive MEO antennas do not require external power sources and rely on natural elements to receive and transmit signals, making them more cost-effective and energy-efficient for short-range communication needs. Both types cater to different market demands based on specific requirements and functionalities.</p></p>
