<p><p>Fiberglass tanks are classified into two main types - Type I and Type II based on their design and intended use. Type I tanks are designed for underground storage of hazardous chemicals and fuels, offering high corrosion resistance and durability. On the other hand, Type II tanks are used for aboveground storage of less hazardous materials, providing moderate corrosion resistance and cost-effectiveness. Both types cater to different industry needs, ensuring safe storage and transportation of liquids.</p></p>
