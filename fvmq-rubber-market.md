<p><p>FVMQ rubber, or Fluorosilicone rubber, is available in three main market types: liquid, solid, and semisolid. Liquid FVMQ is a fluid-like material used for molding and casting applications. Solid FVMQ is a rigid form suitable for extrusion and compression molding. Semisolid FVMQ falls between the other two types, offering a balance of flexibility and strength. Each market type caters to specific applications and requirements within industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
