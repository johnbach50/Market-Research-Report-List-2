Double-sided Thick Copper PCBs have copper on both sides of the board, allowing for more complex layouts and better heat dissipation. Thick Copper Multilayer PCBs have multiple layers of copper with increased thickness, offering enhanced performance and durability for high-power applications.</p></p>
