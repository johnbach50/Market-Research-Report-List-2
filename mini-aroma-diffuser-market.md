<p><p>Mini aroma diffusers are available in two main market types: heating and wind blowing. Heating diffusers use a low level of heat to disperse essential oils into the air, providing a gentle and consistent release of fragrance. Wind blowing diffusers, on the other hand, use a fan or air current to distribute the scents throughout a room. Both types offer a convenient and effective way to enjoy the benefits of aromatherapy in a compact and portable device.</p></p>
