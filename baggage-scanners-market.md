<p><p>Baggage scanners are available in two main types: Channel Scanning Machines and Portable Scanning Machines. Channel Scanning Machines are fixed installations typically found at airports and other high-security areas, where the baggage passes through a channel for scanning. Portable Scanning Machines, on the other hand, are more versatile and can be easily transported to different locations for temporary scanning needs. Both types of baggage scanners play a crucial role in ensuring security and safety by detecting any potentially harmful or prohibited items in baggage.</p></p>
