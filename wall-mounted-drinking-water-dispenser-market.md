<p><p>Wall-mounted drinking water dispensers come in two main types: barrel water dispenser and direct water dispenser. Barrel water dispensers are designed to dispense water from large barrels or jugs. They are typically used in homes or offices where bottled water is preferred. Direct water dispensers, on the other hand, are connected directly to the water supply and filter the water before dispensing it. They are popular in commercial settings where a continuous supply of fresh, filtered water is needed.</p></p>
