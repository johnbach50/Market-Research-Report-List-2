<p><p>Automotive adaptive lighting market includes three main types: Halogen, LED, and Xenon. Halogen lighting is the most common and least expensive option, providing a yellowish light. LED lighting offers increased energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and brighter light output. Xenon lighting, also known as HID (high-intensity discharge) lighting, produces a bright white light that closely resembles natural daylight. Each type of lighting has its own benefits and drawbacks, allowing consumers to choose the option that best suits their needs and preferences.</p></p>
