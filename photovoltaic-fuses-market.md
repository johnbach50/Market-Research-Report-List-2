<p><strong>The Photovoltaic Fuses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photovoltaic fuses are commonly classified into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I fuses are designed for high voltage photovoltaic systems and provide quick protection against overcurrents and short-circuits. They are commonly used in utility-scale solar installations. On the other hand, Type II fuses are used in lower voltage photovoltaic systems and offer protection against overcurrents, reverse currents, and ground faults. They are typically found in residential and commercial solar setups. Each type of fuse serves a specific purpose based on the voltage and scale of the system it is intended for.</p></p>
