<p><strong>The Multi-band Booster Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dual Band</li><li>Tri-band</li><li>Quad-band</li><li>Five-band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multi-band boosters are available in various types such as Dual Band, Tri-band, Quad-band, and Five-band markets. Dual Band boosters operate on two frequency bands, typically 800MHz and 1900MHz. Tri-band boosters cover three bands, including 800MHz, 1900MHz, and AWS. Quad-band boosters work on four bands, which are 700MHz, 850MHz, 1900MHz, and AWS. Five-band boosters cover an additional 1700/2100MHz band on top of the other bands. These different configurations cater to the specific needs and network requirements of different regions and cellular operators.</p></p>
