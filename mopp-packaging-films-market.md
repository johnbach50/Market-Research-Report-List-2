<p><ul><li>Below 50 μm</li><li>51-100 μm</li><li>Above 100μm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MOPP packaging films are categorized based on their thickness into three main types: Below 50 μm, 51-100 μm, and Above 100 μm markets. Films below 50 μm are lightweight and ideal for products that require minimal protection. The 51-100 μm films offer moderate protection and are commonly used for a wide range of products. Films above 100 μm provide heavy-duty protection and are suitable for high-value or fragile items that need extra security during storage or transportation.</p></p>
