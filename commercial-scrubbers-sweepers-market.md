<p><p>Commercial scrubbers and sweepers come in three main types based on their mode of operation: riding type, walking type, and self-action. Riding type machines are large and are operated by a person riding on them, making them suitable for cleaning large areas quickly. Walking type machines are smaller and are pushed or operated manually, making them ideal for smaller spaces or tight areas. Self-action machines are unmanned and operate autonomously, making them ideal for continuous cleaning tasks without human intervention.</p></p>
