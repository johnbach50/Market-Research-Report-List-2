<p><p>Travertine is available in two main market types: Magnesian Travertine and High-Calcium Travertine. Magnesian Travertine contains high levels of magnesium carbonate, giving it a unique color and appearance. This type of travertine is popular for its distinctive veining and swirls. On the other hand, High-Calcium Travertine contains higher levels of calcium carbonate, making it more durable and resistant to weathering. Both types are used in various construction and design applications, but their specific properties make them suitable for different projects.</p></p>
