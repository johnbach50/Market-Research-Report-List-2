<p><strong>The Surface Mount Oscillator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD Oscillator</li><li>SMT Oscillator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface Mount Oscillator Market consists of two main types: SMD Oscillator and SMT Oscillator. SMD Oscillator, also known as Surface Mount Device Oscillator, is a type of oscillator that can be directly mounted onto the surface of a printed circuit board. On the other hand, SMT Oscillator, also known as Surface Mount Technology Oscillator, refers to a type of oscillator that utilizes surface mount technology for its assembly onto a PCB. Both types offer compact size, high reliability, and ease of manufacturing, making them popular choices in the electronics industry.</p></p>
