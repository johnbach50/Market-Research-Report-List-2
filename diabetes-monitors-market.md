<p><strong>The Diabetes Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point Sample Test Based Glucose Monitors</li><li>CGM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Point sample test-based glucose monitors are traditional glucose monitors that require users to manually prick their finger to draw a blood sample for testing. These monitors provide an immediate glucose reading but require multiple tests throughout the day. Continuous Glucose Monitors (CGM) are a newer technology that provides real-time glucose readings through a sensor inserted under the skin. CGM allows for constant monitoring without the need for multiple finger pricks, providing valuable data for better diabetes management.</p></p>
