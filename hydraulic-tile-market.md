<p><p>Hydraulic tiles are commonly classified into two main types: Standard Field Tiles and Ridge Tiles. Standard Field Tiles are typically used as the main flooring option, providing a smooth and durable surface. These tiles come in various shapes, sizes, and designs, catering to different aesthetic preferences. On the other hand, Ridge Tiles are specifically designed for roofing purposes. They are placed along the ridges of roofs to provide protection against water leakage and enhance the overall appearance of the structure. Both types play a significant role in the construction industry.</p></p>
