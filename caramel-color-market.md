<p><strong>The Caramel Color Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class I Caramel Color</li><li>Class II Caramel Color</li><li>Class III Caramel Color</li><li>Class IV Caramel Color</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Caramel color is categorized into four different classes based on its production method and characteristics. Class I caramel color is produced through the heating of carbohydrates with acids or alkalis and is known for its clear appearance and low viscosity. Class II caramel color is created through the heating of carbohydrates with acids and has a slightly darker color and higher viscosity. Class III caramel color is produced through the controlled heating of carbohydrates with ammonia and has a rich brown hue. Class IV caramel color is made by heating carbohydrates with sulfites and produces a black color.</p></p>
