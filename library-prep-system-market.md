<p><p>Library prep systems can be classified into two main types: automatic and fully automatic. In automatic systems, some steps of the library preparation process are carried out manually by the user, while others are automated by the system. On the other hand, in fully automatic systems, all steps of the library preparation process are automated, requiring minimal user intervention. Fully automatic systems are considered to be more efficient and time-saving compared to automatic systems, making them increasingly popular in research and clinical laboratories.</p></p>
