<p><p>Thermal type IR sensors detect infrared radiation emitted by objects based on their temperature, making them ideal for applications like temperature monitoring and thermal imaging. Quantum type IR sensors, on the other hand, utilize quantum mechanics principles and semiconductor technology to detect infrared radiation with high accuracy and sensitivity. These sensors are commonly used in security systems and remote controls. Both thermal and quantum type IR sensors play crucial roles in various industries and applications where detecting infrared radiation is essential.</p></p>
